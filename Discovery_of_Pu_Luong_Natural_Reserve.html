Discovery of Pu Luong Natural Reserve

1 Day Tour
Previous Next × Close

After breakfast a local guide will pick you up at Hoa Ban homestay (or your hotel) and start for the trip to Pu Luong Natural Reserve. On the long way we will stop at a number of amazing observation points to overlook the deep green valleys of the reserve, and you will be able to stop at points on your request. We will have time to try local tea and take photos with local people. After that we will get down into the valley of Phố Đoàn and Cổ lũng villages to experience the local markets. On the way to the villages we will pass by the regional river which the villagers use for agriculture,. We will see the rice fields that are flooded by an authentic method with beautiful water wheels. After all of the this goodness, we will get off from the transport and make a short trekking up to Hieu Village and visit a house of Thai people, take a rest and have a lunch there. After the lunch we will go to swim in Hieu Waterfall. Afterwards we will drive back to Hoa Ban Homestay, Mai Chau and finish the tour. The tour is about 9 hours.


Tour includes:

1. Transportation

2. Local guide

3. Lunch and local snacks

4. Water


Please don't forget to take with you:

1. Sun cream

2. Swimming suite and towel

3. Insect spray

4. Photo camera


We look forward to seeing you on this wonderful tour!
