Discovery of Hoa Bình Lake

1 Day Tour
Previous Next × Close

After the breakfast a local guide and driver will pick us up at Hoa Ban homestay and transfer to the boat dock at Hoa Binh Lake. Hoa Binh Lake as known as Da River has a varied landscape, with terrain divided by many rivers, slots, streams...There are 47 small Islands in the lake, 11 of which are limestone, 116 hectares and 36 island mountain lands with an area of 157.5 hectares. We will float on a boat about one hour towards the Island of a holy shrine - Bà Chúa Thác Bờ Temple.This temple is a place of worship and gratitude to a woman named Dinh Thi Van, she was supporting King Le Loi army during the war. After observing the temple we will continue floating to another island and visit Thác Bờ Cave. Inside the cave there is a large statue of Buddha, avalokitesvara Bodhisattva and the gods ruled this region. The place also has the altar of Uncle Ho. Thac Bo Temple and Thac Bo Cave both have become favorite spiritual tourism destinations of pilgrims, due to their amazing natural scenery. In 2009, Thac Bo cave has been recognized by the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism as a national monument. After that we will stop for lunch in the boat, away from the noise of people, only surrounded by nature and clean water. After lunch and rest you can swim right on the lake ( of course there is the protection of the float in a swimming pool). We will float on our boat more and come back to the dock. From the boat dock our car driver will bring us back to Hoa Ban Homestay. The whole tour is about 9 hours long.


Tour includes:

1. Transportation

2. Local guide

3. Lunch, local tea and snacks

4. Water


Please don't forget to take with you:

1. Sun cream

2. Long pants (because we visit the temples)

3. Swimming suite and towel

4. Hat or umbrella

5. Photo camera


We look forward to seeing you on this wonderful tour!
