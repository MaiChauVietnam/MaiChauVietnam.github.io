Xam Tạng Lake at Nong Luông village

1/2 Day Tour
Previous Next × Close

After the breakfast local guide will pick you up at Hoa Ban Homestay and our tour will start. We will climb up Thung Khe Pass and drive towards the Nong Luong village, the village of Muong and the Thai people. As we climb the mountains, we will stop at a number of observation points to overlook breathtaking Hoa Binh mountains, rice terraces and farmhouses scattered in the landscape. We will continue our way towards Xam Tang lake and enjoy the unique way there. As we arrive we will get off our transport and make a small trekking around the lake through the corn fields and other farms carefully arranged on the hills. When we reach the observation point you will be able to see the the surface of the water with reflection of green mountains, sky and clouds. On the lakeside we will stop for a picnic with local snacks and tea. After an hour or so we'll go back to Hoa Ban homestay. On our way we will stop at Mai Chau view point to see the Mai Chau valley. The tour is about 5 hours long.


Tour includes:

1. Transportation

2. Local guide

3. Local tea and snacks

4. Water


Please don't forget to take with you:

1. Sun cream

2. Hat or umbrella

3. Insect spray

4. Photo camera


We look forward to seeing you on this wonderful tour!
