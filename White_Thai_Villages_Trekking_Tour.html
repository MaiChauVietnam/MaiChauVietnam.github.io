White Thai Villages Trekking Tour

1/2 Day Tour
Previous Next Ã— Close

Mai Chau valley is located in mountainous area in the North East of Vietnam. The ethnic group living here is the White Thai people. To learn more about the culture here you will experience the walking program of visiting 6 villages of Mai Chau.

Local guide will pick you up at Hoa Ban homestay and we will start walking around the Lac village - the main touristic spot in Mai Chau. Then we will walk through other villages between rice fields and bamboo forest, visit the local museum of Thai Culture and take a rest next to a small waterfall. After that we will walk to Mai Chau town market where you can buy some necessary items or fresh seasonal fruits from local farmers. We will continue our walk around the rest two villages, taking pictures of beautiful scenery and daily life of Thai people. Tour duration is about 4 hours.


Tour includes:

1. Local guide

3. Local tea and snacks

4. Water

5. Tickets for sightseeing


Please don't forget to take with you:

1. Sun cream

2. Hat or umbrella

3. Insect spray

4. Photo camera


We look forward to seeing you on this wonderful tour!
