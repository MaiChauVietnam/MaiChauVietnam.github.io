The Loop

1/2 Day Tour
Previous Next × Close

After the breakfast or in the afternoon a local guide will meet you at Hoa Ban homestay and our adventure will start towards Mai Chau view point. We will stop there to capture the contrast of the flat rice paddies and towering mountains in our memory and cameras. We will continue driving through the mountains of Thung Khe Pass and stop for a break with local tea and snacks at another view point. While your body will relax on bamboo floor at local style restaurant your eyes explore the valley and mountain expanse. After that we'll go down to the valley we've just seen.The narrow road passes through the picturesque villages and corn plantations. Buffalo families will greet us with their lazily chewing faces. These cozy landscapes will suddenly give way to a breathtaking view of Hoa Binh Lake. This vast lake overflowed in the middle of the mountains and has a depth about 100 meters. Try to imagine this depth when you look at the special color of the surface of the lake. The Mường and Thai people who live on the lakeside are known for their fishing skills. We can see their fishponds float between islands on the lake.Toward the end of the trip, we will take a refreshing dip in the Go Lao waterfall.After all that fun and experience we will go back to the starting point at Hoa Ban homestay and that's how our loop finishes. Tour is about 4 hours long. 


Tour includes:

1. Transportation

2. Local guide

3. Local tea and snacks

4. Water


Please don't forget to take with you:

1. Sun cream

2. Swimming suite and towel

3. Insect spray

4. Photo camera


We look forward to seeing you on this wonderful tour!
